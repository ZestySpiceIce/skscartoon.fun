About

Funding Real Art via Crypto

What is $SKS?

$SKS is a Solana memecoin created by the community to support @skscartoon, a small-time cartoonist from Australia known for sharp political satire.

How It Works

Every trade generates fees that flow directly to the artist's wallet. No intermediaries, no cuts — just pure community-powered support for real art.

Community Driven

Built by fans, for fans. The SKS community is wholesome, supportive, and united by a shared mission: help a talented artist thrive.

"Some awesome people in the crypto community made a meme coin based on my cartoons to support my work. Honestly, I don't really understand meme coins — I have no idea how they're made or how they work. But even I can see this is a super creative way to help creators on X. Maybe one day I'll look back and think this was the little push that let me become a full-time cartoonist. Who knows? Thank you so much, guys — it means a lot!"

— @skscartoon
Get Started

How to Buy $SKS

New to crypto? No problem. Follow these simple steps.

1

Set Up a Wallet

Download Phantom Wallet — it's free and takes 2 minutes.

  • Go to phantom.app
  • Download the app or browser extension
  • Create a new wallet
  • Write down your 12-word recovery phrase (never share it!)
2

Get Some SOL

You need SOL (Solana's currency) to buy $SKS.

  • Easy way: Buy SOL directly in Phantom using card/Apple Pay
  • Alternative: Buy on Coinbase/Binance, send to your Phantom
  • Start with $15-20 (plenty for your first buy)
3

Buy $SKS

Swap your SOL for $SKS on Bags.fm or Jupiter.

  • Go to the buy link below
  • Connect your Phantom wallet
  • Enter amount of SOL (keep 0.01 for fees)
  • Click swap and confirm — done!
